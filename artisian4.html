HOLIDAY / WEEKEND SHIPPING

We typically use FedEx Ground for shipments. While we estimate seven days for a shipment to arrive on the East Coast, this is completely subject to FedEx shipping traffic,
whether FedEx has service interruptions, and just whether FedEx can access the address to which you have had product shipped. Please email or call us if you have questions.

ORDER PROCESSING AND SHIPPING

We typically ship your order within one business day. Sometimes it can take longer, most often during the busy holiday season. We do not ship on weekends or company
holidays. We do our best to get your order out to you as soon as possible because we wouldn't want to go a day without coconut butter or cashew butter, and neither should
you. Just keep in mind that we are people packing your order and we can only pack it as fast as people can :)
You should receive an email when your order is shipped and this should include a tracking number. You can use this tracking number to follow your package. You can also
contact our Customer Service if you have questions about your shipment.
We use USPS and FedEx Ground to ship. How long will it take to get your order? Usually between 2 and 10 days domestically. We ship from Oakland, California, so you'll get
it faster if you're on the West Coast than if you're in New England. If a package can't be delivered due to an error entering your shipping address, Artisana is not responsible for any additional fees that may incur in order to reroute or reship
the package. If you choose not to have the package re-routed or re-shipped, we will refund you for the order MINUS the costs associated with shipping.
