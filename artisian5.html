CAN I BAKE WITH CASHEW
BUTTER? THREE RECIPES
THAT SAYS YES
Semptember 10,2018

Some people ask us if they can sub our nut butters for dairy butter. We wouldn't quite recommend subbing it one for one in, say,
your grandma's sugar cookie recipe. But we would recommend checking out all the new recipes coming online that have been
created specifically with nut butters in mind!

Here are three of our favorite baked good recipes that use cashew butter for an amazingly sweet and rich flavor without refined
sugar or dairy!

BAKERITA'S PALEO BLONDIES

Gooey, delicious, and paleo, with a vegan option! Don't skimp on the raspberries — they really take the recipe up a notch. Pro
Tip? Store the leftovers in the freezer for an amazing chewy treat.

FLORA + VINO'S WHITE BEAN BLONDIES

Gluten free, vegan, and refined sugar free — and extremely satisfying! We love that the white beans add extra fiber but no
"bean-y" taste. You could feel good eating these hearty little treats for breakfast

RACHAEL GOOD EATS CASHEW COOKIE SKILLET

If you haven't yet seen this amazing goodie, you haven't been on the internet in the last year :) It's quickly become a classic and
as soon as you make it, you'll see why.
